The hassle of recurring office tasks

You’ll probably know what we mean; giving some love to the vegetation at your office, get all the dishes in the dishwasher, getting some groceries for the team etc. You could probably name a few more. It’s those small recurring tasks nobody really likes but need to be done, preferably in an honest manner, dividing the workload equally..

Naggy will help you out

No hassle. Just one member that will set-up some recurring tasks and people that should be equally responsible. Naggy will do the rest. It will send text messages to remind people about their responsibilities for tasks at the right time and at the right location.

Naggy’s real

Think about that moment when people ask you to take responsibility for some small tasks and both of you feeling a little awkward when that happens. Naggy will help you out. It has some personality, asking you to do stuff with a more positive tone of voice while sometimes being annoyed and asking you to finally take care of that freaking task.
